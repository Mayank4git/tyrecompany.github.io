Company Profile

Superking Manufacturers (Tyre) Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 1982 and is engaged in the manufacturing and exporting of Agricultural Tyres, Two Wheeler & Three Wheeler Tyres, Flotation Implement Tyres, Golf & Go-Kart Tyres, ADV Tyres, etc.

We have a sophisticated manufacturing unit equipped with cutting edge technology tools for delivering products in compliance with the prevalent market trends. Our quality control unit is loaded with the most efficient and latest equipment for the assurance of an optimum level of quality. We have an efficient Research and Development department for incorporating new trends and techniques in our manufacturing process for increasing the productivity. Our spacious warehouse is used for storing bulk orders systematically. We have a dedicated team of members comprising of technicians, researchers, quality controllers, engineers and warehouse personnel, who work in coordination with each other.
