About us

Who are we

Superking Manufacturers (Tyre) Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 1982 and is engaged in the manufacturing and exporting of Agricultural Tyres, Two Wheeler & Three Wheeler Tyres, Flotation Implement Tyres, Golf & Go-Kart Tyres, ADV Tyres, etc. We have an efficient Research and Development department for incorporating new trends and techniques in our manufacturing process for increasing the productivity. Our spacious warehouse is used for storing bulk orders systematically.
